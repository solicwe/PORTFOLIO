Event Date

June 2025

Role

Committee: Venue Team

Event Overview

The Faculty Freshmen Orientation is a major annual event designed to welcome new engineering students to Srinakharinwirot University. As a member of the Venue Team Committee, I was responsible for the logistical foundation of the event, ensuring the location was safe, organized, and ready for all planned activities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assisted in event setup, stage preparation, and dismantling
  • Managed inter-department coordination to ensure smooth operations
  • Oversaw venue logistics, seating arrangements, and equipment readiness
  • Collaborated with other teams to solve on-site logistical challenges
