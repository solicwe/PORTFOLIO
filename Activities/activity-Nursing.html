Nursing College Workshop

Date

Aug 16-17, 2025

Role

Assistant Instructor & 3D Modeler

Organization

Srinakharinwirot University

Overview

This workshop aimed to introduce Nursing College students and staff to Metaverse technologies. I served as an Assistant Instructor, helping bridge the gap between technical 3D modeling tools and educational applications in nursing.

Key Responsibilities

  • Guided participants in creating 3D classroom models using Blender
  • Provided hands-on technical support during the workshop sessions
  • Assisted with on-site hardware and software setup
  • Facilitated the integration of 3D assets into the virtual learning environment
