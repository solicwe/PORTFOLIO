Inam Cafe & Bar Metaverse

Project Date

Dec 5-6, 2025

Role

Metaverse Developer & 3D Modeler

Organization

Srinakharinwirot University

Overview

This project involved creating a digital twin of Inam Cafe & Bar for a Metaverse environment. As a key developer, I focused on modeling the cafeteria zone to create an immersive and realistic virtual experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed the 3D cafeteria zone using Blender
  • Assisted the team in scene composition and asset placement
  • Optimized 3D models for web-based metaverse performance
  • Ensured texture and lighting accuracy to match the real-world location
