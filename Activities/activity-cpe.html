CPE Family Connection 2025

Event Date

Aug 13 & 22, 2025

Role

Head of Welfare Team

Department

Computer Engineering

Overview

The CPE Family Connection is an annual networking event organized by the Computer Engineering Department at Srinakharinwirot University. As Head of the Welfare Team, I was responsible for ensuring the comfort and well-being of all attendees throughout the event.

Key Responsibilities

  • Managed catering services for over 200+ attendees
  • Coordinated logistics for food distribution and dining areas
  • Ensured smooth coordination between welfare team members
  • Handled dietary requirements and special requests
  • Maintained event cleanliness and organization
  • Provided immediate support for attendee needs

Leadership Skills Developed

  • Team management and delegation
  • Problem-solving under pressure
  • Budget management and resource allocation
  • Cross-team communication and coordination
  • Event planning and execution
